How to play color-linez

Start by clicking on an empty box to add colored circles.

Click on a colored circle and then on a blank square to add more circles.

Once you have have most of the boxes filled with colored dots, you can move them one space at a time to make your lines of five.

Don't wait too long because new colored circles may continue to populate the board while you're trying to move yours into a line.

The goal is to make a row of at least five of the same color.

You can move other circles out of the way by moving them one spot to make space for the matching color.

Once you have made a row of (at least) five, those circles will disappear from the board and you will get a point for each colored circle in the line.

Happy playing!
